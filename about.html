Our Story

At MisFits Tattoo Collective, we believe in the power of self-expression through ink. Founded by The Gallons, our studio is a sanctuary for those who seek to transform their ideas into unique tattoo art. With over 30 years of experience in the industry, Both artists have honed their skills in various styles, specializing in quality traditional designs

Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment where clients can feel comfortable and confident about their tattoos. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to high-quality work. Every tattoo is a collaboration between the artist and the client, ensuring that each piece is not only a work of art but a meaningful expression of individuality.
